About IEEE VIT Student Branch

IEEE-Vidyalankar Insitute of Technology Student Branch was formed in 2009. We started gaining traction in 2011 and have been consistent in conducting various events since then.

IEEE-VIT bagged the Best Student Chapter Award in IEEE Bombay Section for the year 2014-15, and was one of the 11 nominees for the IEEE India Council Outstanding Student Chapter Award.

Currently, IEEE VIT serves over 180 active student members and many senior members in the institute.
